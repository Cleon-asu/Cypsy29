Study Overview / Visao Geral

science

English Summary

This work tested persona prompts in an iterated Split or Steal game where agents interacted with a virtual human. Four open models were evaluated at two temperatures while the virtual human used GPT-4.1 mini. Cooperation dominated outcomes, with model and persona differences shaping strategy.

translate

Resumo em Portugues

Este trabalho avaliou prompts de persona num jogo iterado de Split or Steal, onde agentes interagiram com um humano virtual. Foram testados quatro modelos abertos em duas temperaturas, enquanto o humano virtual usou GPT-4.1 mini. A cooperacao dominou os resultados, com diferencas de modelo e persona a influenciarem a estrategia.

Key Findings / Resultados Principais

handshake

Cooperation First

Mutual Split outcomes occurred in roughly 74% of rounds across sessions.

model_training

Model Differences

phi4 and Ministral stayed more cooperative, while Gemma models varied more by context.

person

Persona Effect

Prosocial and Principled personas were more cooperative; Analytical personas exploited more.

forum

Dialogue Signal

Friendship language aligned with Split, while money and vengeance terms aligned with Steal.
