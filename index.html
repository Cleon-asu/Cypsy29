Abstract

Introduction: This project explores whether people treat an AI virtual human as a true social partner when the agent is embodied with a realistic face in virtual reality. We compare responses across Human-VH and Human-Human gameplay conditions to examine social presence, communication behavior, and interaction quality. The main idea is to understand when intelligent virtual humans can approximate human-like social engagement.
